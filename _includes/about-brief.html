About me

Dr. Avinash Karn holds a Ph.D. in Plant, Insect, and Microbial Sciences from the University of Missouri-Columbia and a Bachelor of Science in Agricultural Science from Truman State University in Kirksville, Missouri. He specializes in plant breeding, statistical genetics and computational biology, and has an excellent knowledge and experience in conducting big analysis on genotypic and phenotypic data sets. He intends to use his education and training in pursuing a career in a research organization that is involved to improve human nutrition, health and environment for both developed and developing communities.
